Premium Flooring Solutions

Siberian Larch flooring brings natural warmth and timeless elegance to any interior space. Its exceptional hardness and beautiful grain patterns make it an ideal choice for high-traffic areas.

Our flooring boards are carefully kiln dried to optimal moisture content and precision-milled for perfect fit and finish. The natural properties of Siberian Larch ensure your floors will look beautiful for generations.
