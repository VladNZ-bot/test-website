Siberian Larch Cladding

Beautiful, durable exterior cladding solutions

Premium Cladding Solutions

Siberian Larch cladding offers exceptional performance and stunning aesthetics for exterior facades. Its natural beauty and durability make it the premier choice for both residential and commercial projects.

Our cladding profiles are precision-machined from carefully selected Siberian Larch, ensuring consistent quality and beautiful results. Available in various profiles to suit your architectural vision.

Weather Resistance

Natural resin content provides excellent protection against moisture, UV rays, and New Zealand's harsh coastal conditions.

Low Maintenance

Can be left to weather naturally to a silver-grey patina, or oiled/stained to maintain the golden honey tones.

Versatile Profiles

Available in shiplap, weatherboard, vertical, and custom profiles to achieve your desired architectural look.
