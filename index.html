Siberian Larch Advantages

Siberian Larch's high stability, density and versatility makes it desirable in many building projects.

VERSATILITY

Siberian Larch is very dense at 650kg - 700kg per m3 when dried. It is hard wearing and perfect for exposed elevations or high traffic areas. Suitable for both commercial and residential projects, from flooring to facades of houses, decor of interiors, decor of landscape elements.

DURABILITY

Siberian Larch has a maintenance free service life. The presence of resin in the wood creates a high resistance to decay and rot. The high density means that it is more difficult for decaying organisms to penetrate the wood. It makes it one of the toughest and most durable softwoods.
