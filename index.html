Siberian Larch Advantages

Natural Durability

Grown in harsh Siberian conditions, the timber develops exceptional density and natural resistance to rot, decay, and insects.

Weather Resistant

High resin content provides natural protection against rain, snow, and UV damage without chemical treatments.

Fire Resistant

Dense grain structure gives Siberian Larch a higher fire resistance rating than many other softwoods.

Beautiful Grain

Stunning natural grain patterns and warm honey tones that age gracefully to a distinguished silver-grey.

Eco-Friendly

Sustainably harvested from managed forests with full chain of custody certification.

Cost Effective

Premium quality at competitive prices thanks to our direct sourcing model from Siberian suppliers.
