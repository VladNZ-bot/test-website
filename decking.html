Premium Decking Solutions

Siberian Larch decking is the perfect choice for your outdoor living areas. With its natural durability and beautiful grain patterns, it creates stunning deck spaces that last for decades.

Our decking boards are kiln dried and precisely milled to ensure consistent quality and easy installation. The natural oils in Siberian Larch provide excellent resistance to weathering, making it ideal for New Zealand's varied climate.
